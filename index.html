Summer Music Festival 2025

Join us for a weekend of amazing music, food, and fun!

Event Details

Date: August 15 - 17, 2025

Location: Riverfront Park, Springfield

Experience three days of live performances by top artists, local food vendors, craft beer, and activities for all ages.

Schedule

  • Friday, Aug 15: 5 PM - Opening Ceremony & Main Stage Concert
  • Saturday, Aug 16: 12 PM - Workshops & Local Bands | 6 PM - Headliner Concert
  • Sunday, Aug 17: 11 AM - Family Activities | 4 PM - Closing Act

Location & Parking

Riverfront Park is located downtown Springfield, easily accessible by public transit and car.

Parking available at the North Lot and South Garage. Shuttle service runs every 30 minutes from nearby hotels.
